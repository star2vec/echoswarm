## SOP Update — Inadequate Verifiable Content
- **Rule:** Include at least two verifiable data points (e.g., satellite timestamp, confirmed road closure, authority name, and a specific evacuation route identifier) to enable Skeptical agents to self-validate without needing external confirmation.
- **Rule:** Specify evacuation routes as a sequence of precise street intersections (e.g., "from Plaça de Vicente Blanch to Carrer d'Ausiàs March") to minimize degradation of route details through word-of-mouth hops.
- **Rule:** Provide an alternative shelter location with a non-zero capacity, clearly stated as a precise street intersection, to give agents a verifiable and actionable evacuation target.
